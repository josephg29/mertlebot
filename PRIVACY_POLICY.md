# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Mertle Bot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Project Descriptions**: The hardware project ideas you describe to the Service
- **Skill Level**: Your selected skill level (Monkey, Novice, Builder, Hacker, Expert)
- **Age**: Your selected age (used for age-appropriate instructions)
- **API Key**: If you configure an Anthropic API key, it is stored locally in your browser

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **IP Address**: For rate limiting and security purposes
- **Browser Type**: For compatibility and optimization
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns
- **Technical Data**: Device information, operating system, and screen resolution

### 2.3 Local Storage
We use browser local storage to:
- Save your project history
- Store your theme preferences
- Remember your skill level and age settings
- Cache API key (if provided)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Generate build guides based on your project descriptions
- Provide age-appropriate and skill-level-appropriate instructions
- Improve and optimize the Service
- Prevent abuse through rate limiting
- Maintain security and prevent fraudulent activity
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Services
We share information with third-party services only as necessary:

- **Anthropic API**: Your project descriptions are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses
- **Wokwi API**: When generating simulations, project details are sent to Wokwi's API

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

### 4.3 Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred.

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Server-Side Data
- **IP Addresses**: Retained for 24 hours for rate limiting purposes
- **Usage Logs**: Retained for 30 days for debugging and optimization

### 5.2 Client-Side Data
- **Local Storage**: Persists until you clear your browser data
- **API Keys**: Stored locally in your browser only

### 5.3 Third-Party Data
We do not control how third-party services (Anthropic, Wokwi) retain data sent to them. Please review their privacy policies.

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Same-origin CORS policy
- Security headers (CSP, no x-powered-by)
- No persistent user accounts or passwords

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access**: Request information about what data we have
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mertle.bot.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ.

## 10. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites (Amazon for parts, Wokwi for simulations). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

- Email: privacy@mertle.bot
- GitHub: https://github.com/josephg29/mertlebot/issues

## 13. California Privacy Rights

California residents have the right to request information regarding the disclosure of their personal information to third parties for direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please contact us at privacy@mertle.bot.

## 14. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our legal basis for processing is your consent and our legitimate interest in providing the Service.
