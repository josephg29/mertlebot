# Privacy Policy - Simple Version

**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

## What Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Name, email, and password when you create an account
- Profile information you choose to add
- Content you create or share in the app
- Messages you send

### Information We Collect Automatically
- Device information (type, OS version)
- How you use the app (features, time spent)
- Approximate location (from IP address)
- Technical data (IP address, browser type)

### Google Account Integration
If you sign in with Google:
- We get your name, email, and profile picture from Google
- We never see or store your Google password
- You can disconnect Google anytime in your Google account settings

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and improve the app
- To create and manage your account
- To personalize your experience
- To communicate with you
- To keep the app secure
- To comply with laws

## Information Sharing
We don't sell your personal information. We only share it:
- With your permission
- With service providers who help us run the app
- If required by law
- If we're involved in a business transfer

## Your Choices
- You can update your account information anytime
- You can delete your account
- You can opt out of marketing emails
- You can manage Google permissions in your Google account

## Data Security
We use industry-standard security measures, but no system is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy
Our app is not for children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. We'll notify you of important changes.
