## Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **AI API costs**: Implement caching, rate limits, cost monitoring
- **Scalability**: Use serverless architecture, auto-scaling
- **Security**: Regular audits, bug bounty program

### Market Risks
- **Competition**: Focus on unique features (wiring diagrams, simulations)
- **Adoption**: Strong educational focus, community building
- **Monetization**: Multiple revenue streams, freemium model

### Operational Risks
- **Team**: Start small, remote-first, contractor network
- **Legal**: Early legal counsel, clear terms, insurance
- **Funding**: Bootstrap initially, seek funding at traction milestones
